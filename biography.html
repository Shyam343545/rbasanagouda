Life Journey

A timeline of significant milestones and achievements

1975 - Early Life

Born in a humble family in Maski constituency, Karnataka. Developed early interest in community service and social welfare.

1995 - Educational Foundation

Completed graduation in Political Science and Law. Actively participated in student politics and community development programs.

2000 - Political Entry

Joined local politics with focus on grassroots development. Started working on rural infrastructure and education initiatives.

2008 - First Electoral Victory

Won first election as MLA with overwhelming support from the constituency. Focused on agricultural development and water conservation.

2013 - Re-election

Re-elected with increased majority. Launched major infrastructure projects including road development and healthcare facilities.

2018 - Third Term

Continued mandate from people. Focused on digital governance, women empowerment, and sustainable development initiatives.

2023 - Current Term

Currently serving fourth term with focus on smart governance, environmental conservation, and youth development programs.
